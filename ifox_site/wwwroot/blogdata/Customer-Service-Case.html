🛠️ Customer Service Case Summaries with Copilot

Customer service teams handle large volumes of support cases every day. Quickly understanding the history of a case is critical for delivering fast and accurate resolutions. However, manually reviewing long case timelines can slow agents down and impact customer satisfaction.

Dynamics 365 Copilot transforms service operations with AI-powered case summarization , enabling agents to instantly understand customer issues, previous interactions, and next steps.

Copilot automatically generates concise, contextual summaries of customer service cases — helping agents resolve issues faster and more efficiently.

The Challenge with Traditional Case Reviews

In many service environments, agents must manually read through case notes, emails, chat transcripts, and activity logs before taking action. This process is time-consuming and prone to oversight.

Common pain points include:

  • Long case histories to review
  • Delayed response times
  • Inconsistent case understanding
  • Agent onboarding challenges
  • Reduced first-contact resolution rates

These inefficiencies can negatively affect both agent productivity and customer experience.

How Copilot Case Summaries Work

Copilot uses generative AI and natural language processing to analyze all activities associated with a customer service case.

The AI reviews:

  • Case notes and comments
  • Email conversations
  • Chat transcripts
  • Call logs
  • Status changes and activities

It then produces a clear, structured summary highlighting the most important information.
